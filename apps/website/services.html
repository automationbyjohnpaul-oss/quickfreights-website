What We Offer

Our Services

Comprehensive logistics solutions tailored for importers and exporters across Nigeria.

Seamless Port Clearance

Our team handles every aspect of the clearance process — from customs documentation and duty assessment to regulatory compliance and final release. We work directly at Onne Port so your cargo moves without unnecessary delays.

  • Customs documentation preparation and submission
  • Duty and tariff calculation and payment
  • Regulatory agency liaison (SON, NAFDAC)
  • Terminal handling and delivery coordination

Freight Forwarding

Whether FCL or LCL, Quick Freights Global ensures efficient movement of goods from Onne Port and other maritime hubs to any destination across Nigeria.

  • FCL and LCL freight management
  • Shipping line coordination and booking
  • Bill of Lading processing and verification
  • Inland transportation arrangement

End-to-End Logistics

From the moment your vessel berths at Onne Port to the final delivery at your warehouse, Quick Freights Global manages every step.

  • Port-to-warehouse transportation
  • Warehousing and temporary storage
  • Inventory tracking and status reporting
  • Last-mile delivery coordination

Strategic Hub Operations

Our dual-location presence gives us a competitive edge. Port Harcourt (Onne) provides direct port access, while Onitsha positions us at the commercial heart of the East.

  • Onne Port direct operations centre
  • Eagle Plaza, Onitsha commercial hub
  • Eastern corridor route expertise
  • Local knowledge and regulatory relationships
