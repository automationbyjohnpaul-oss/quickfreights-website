Our Services

Complete Logistics Solutions

Everything you need to move cargo efficiently across Nigerian ports.

Customs Clearance & Brokerage

Professional customs brokerage ensuring your cargo clears Nigerian ports quickly and compliantly. We handle all documentation, duty calculations, and regulatory liaison so you don't have to.

Who needs this?

Importers bringing goods through Onne, Apapa, or Tin Can Island ports.

  • PAAR processing & Form M documentation
  • Duty calculation & payment facilitation
  • SONCAP / NAFDAC regulatory clearance
  • Direct liaison with Nigeria Customs Service
  • Demurrage-free clearance priority

International Freight Forwarding

Reliable air and sea freight solutions connecting your supply chain across continents. From origin pickup to port delivery, we manage the entire journey.

Who needs this?

Businesses importing or exporting goods internationally by sea or air.

  • Sea freight (FCL & LCL) worldwide
  • Air freight with priority handling
  • Consolidation & deconsolidation services
  • Port-to-port & door-to-door options
  • Competitive freight rates & space guarantees

Integrated Logistics Solutions

End-to-end logistics coordination covering planning, documentation, port handling, inland transport, and final delivery — one partner, one point of contact.

Who needs this?

Companies seeking a single logistics partner for their entire supply chain.

  • Full-cycle shipment planning & tracking
  • Multi-modal transport coordination
  • Inland container haulage across Nigeria
  • Customs brokerage bundled with forwarding
  • Dedicated account manager for corporate clients

Warehousing & Distribution

Secure storage and efficient distribution keeping your goods safe and your supply chain moving. Strategically located facilities near major ports and commercial centers.

Who needs this?

Importers needing temporary storage or nationwide distribution of cleared goods.

  • Bonded & non-bonded warehouse options
  • Inventory management & stock reporting
  • Cross-docking & transloading
  • Last-mile delivery across Nigerian states
  • Short & long-term storage plans
