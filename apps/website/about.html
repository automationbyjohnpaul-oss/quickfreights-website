About Quick Freights Global Limited

A trusted name in clearing, forwarding, and logistics across Nigeria.

Our Story

Quick Freights Global Limited was established as a subsidiary of Bondex Bridge Multinational Terminal Limited with a clear mission: to remove the bottlenecks and frustrations that businesses face at Nigerian ports. We saw an industry burdened by delays, opaque processes, and unreliable service — and we set out to change that.

From our strategic bases in Port Harcourt (Onne) and Onitsha, we have grown into one of the most trusted clearing and forwarding agencies serving the eastern industrial corridor. Our deep understanding of customs regulations, combined with strong relationships at the ports, allows us to deliver what we promise: very fast delivery.

We are not just agents; we are logistics partners committed to the success of every importer, exporter, and trader who puts their trust in us.

Our Mission

To provide seamless, transparent, and rapid port clearance and logistics services that empower businesses to thrive without worrying about supply chain disruptions.

Our Vision

To become Nigeria's most reliable clearing and forwarding partner, known for speed, integrity, and unmatched customer satisfaction.
