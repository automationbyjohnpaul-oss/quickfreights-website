Who We Are

Quick Freights Global Limited is a premier clearing and forwarding agency operating across Nigeria's busiest ports. A subsidiary of Bondex Bridge Multinational Terminal Limited, we bring decades of combined logistics experience to every shipment we handle.

From Onne Port to your warehouse, we manage the entire clearance process — documentation, duty processing, regulatory compliance, and final delivery — so you can focus on your business.
