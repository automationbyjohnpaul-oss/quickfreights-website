# Overview

The Quick Freights Platform is a digital logistics management system developed for **Quick Freights Global Limited**, a subsidiary of **Bondex Bridge Multinational Terminal Limited**.

The platform replaces manual cargo processing with a secure, centralized, and scalable workflow for Bill of Lading (B/L) submissions, shipment tracking, document management, customer communication, and operational record keeping.

The long-term vision is to build a modern logistics platform that improves operational efficiency while delivering a better customer experience through automation and digital self-service.

# Business Objectives

The Quick Freights Platform aims to:

- Digitize cargo documentation and submission.
- Reduce manual processing and administrative workload.
- Improve shipment visibility.
- Automate customer communication.
- Provide reliable operational records.
- Build a scalable foundation for future logistics services.

# Engineering Principles

The platform is built around the following principles:

- Single Source of Truth (SSOT)
- Modular architecture
- Clear separation of responsibilities
- Maintainability
- Security by design
- Documentation-first development
- Measured performance optimization
- Incremental enhancement over unnecessary refactoring
