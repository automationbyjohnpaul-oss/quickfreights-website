## Brand Guidelines

Version: 1.0
Last Updated: July 2026

---

# Purpose

This document defines the official visual identity and communication standards for Quick Freights Global Limited.

Its purpose is to ensure consistency across:

- Website
- Marketing materials
- Social media
- Email communications
- Future applications
- Printed materials

No visual or branding changes should be made without updating this document.

---

# Brand Position

Quick Freights Global Limited is positioned as a professional Nigerian Clearing, Forwarding and Logistics company specialising in customs clearance, cargo handling, freight forwarding and supply chain support.

The brand should communicate:

- Trust
- Professionalism
- Speed
- Transparency
- Reliability
- Experience

---

Quick Freights should always feel:

Professional

# Brand Promise

> Moving Cargo. Building Trust.

Every customer interaction should reinforce this promise.

---

# Logo

The Quick Freights logo is the primary visual identifier of the company.

The preferred version is the full-colour logo on a white or light background.

---

# Logo Usage

Always:

✓ Maintain original proportions

✓ Keep sufficient white space

✓ Use high-resolution artwork

✓ Use official colours

Never:

✗ Stretch

✗ Rotate

✗ Recolour

✗ Add effects

✗ Place over busy backgrounds

# Photography Style

Images should feature:

Shipping containers

Ports

Cargo

Warehouses

Logistics operations

Customs processes

Professional staff

Where people appear:

Prefer authentic Nigerian professionals where appropriate to reflect the company's customer base and operating environment.

Avoid generic stock imagery that feels disconnected from the local logistics industry.

# Spacing Philosophy

Whitespace communicates professionalism.

Layouts should breathe.

Avoid:

Crowded sections

Large unexplained gaps

Inconsistent spacing

Spacing should follow the design system.

---

# Customer Language

Use customer-focused language.

Preferred

Cargo Portal

Cargo Processing

Customs Clearance

Freight Forwarding

Cargo Documentation

Tracking ID

Customer Support

Avoid unnecessary internal terminology.

---

# Tone of Voice

Professional

Helpful

Confident

Clear

Respectful

Every sentence should be understandable to both experienced importers and first-time customers.

Avoid jargon whenever possible.

---

# Accessibility

Maintain sufficient colour contrast.

Provide:

Alternative text

Keyboard accessibility

Visible focus states

Readable font sizes

Semantic HTML

Accessibility is part of the brand.

---

# Marketing Principles

The website should always answer three customer questions:

Can I trust this company?

Can they solve my problem?

How do I get started?

Every page should move customers toward taking action.

---

# Brand Experience

Visitors should leave the website with the impression that Quick Freights is:

Professional

Established

Reliable

Easy to work with

Customer-focused

Digitally modern

This experience is more important than visual decoration.
